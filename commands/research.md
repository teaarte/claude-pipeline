# Research Command

You are running a **research session** for:

**Topic:** $ARGUMENTS

---

## Instructions

Read `CLAUDE.md` to understand the current stack and existing dependencies.

Then research the topic with the following goals:
1. Find the best solution/library/approach for **this specific project's stack**
2. Compare top 2-3 options
3. Give a clear recommendation

Structure your output as:

## Research: [topic]

### Context
[What we're trying to solve, why research is needed]

### Options Compared

**Option 1: [name]**
- What it does
- Pros for our stack
- Cons
- Bundle impact (if frontend)
- TypeScript quality
- Maintenance status

**Option 2: [name]**
[same format]

### Recommendation
**Use [X]** because [specific reasoning for our project].

### Integration Path
[How to add it, what to change, rough effort estimate]

### Risks & Alternatives
[What to watch out for, what to use if recommendation doesn't work out]
